The Origin

We kept looking for something
that didn't exist.

Not just a book box. Something that actually understood the specific kind of reader we are — the one who annotates her favorites, who romanticizes everything including her inbox, who wants the unboxing to feel like the opening chapter of the book inside.

"We wanted something wrapped in tissue paper like a gift from your most unhinged best friend. So we built it."

Pretty Little Red Flags is a curated dark romance book box for the girls who read the red flags and ask for more details anyway. Every box is hand-selected, tissue-wrapped, and wax-sealed — because the packaging is part of the story.

We are not a corporate operation. We are readers who got tired of waiting for the thing we wanted to exist. This is for the girls who feel seen by morally gray men in fiction, who cry at five-star endings, who send friends book recommendations with zero content warnings and a casual "it gets better after chapter four."

You know who you are. Welcome home.
