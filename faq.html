♥ FAQ

Tiny details.
Big red flag energy.

How does ordering work? +

After checkout via Stripe, you'll be directed to our Reader Questionnaire so we can learn your preferences and curate your box with care.

Is this a subscription? +

We're starting with a limited 10-box Founder Drop. Monthly subscriptions may open after the first drop sells out — join our mailing list or follow on social to stay in the loop.

Can I choose my spice level? +

Yes — the Reader Questionnaire lets you share your spice level, hard no topics, favorite tropes, reading style, and aesthetic preferences. We curate around your answers.

What about snacks or treats? +

We collect allergy and sensitivity notes in the Reader Questionnaire. Sealed treats are used whenever possible. If you have specific concerns, you can let us know in the form.

When does my box ship? +

Boxes are curated and packed once your Reader Questionnaire is complete. You'll receive a shipping notification as soon as your box is on its way.

What's the difference between the boxes? +

The Blind Date is a one-time mystery book with a trope label and small goodies. The Founder Box is our signature drop — limited to 10, with a curated book, tabs, candle/treat, and themed extras. The Deluxe Drama Box is our premium tier with a premium candle, collectible item, and upgraded packaging.

Can I gift a box to someone? +

Yes! All of our boxes make perfect gifts. Just enter the recipient's shipping address at checkout and we'll handle the rest. They'll fill out their own Reader Questionnaire so the curation is personal.
