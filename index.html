♥ FAQ

Tiny details.
Big red flag energy.

How does ordering work?+

After checkout, you'll be directed to our Reader Questionnaire so we can learn your preferences and curate your box with care.

Is this a subscription?+

We're starting with a limited 10-box Founder Drop. Monthly subscriptions may open after the first drop sells out.

Can I choose my spice level?+

Yes. The Reader Questionnaire lets you share your spice level, hard no topics, favorite tropes, reading style, and aesthetic preferences.

What about snacks or treats?+

We collect allergy and sensitivity notes in the Reader Questionnaire. Sealed treats are used whenever possible.

When does my box ship?+

Boxes are curated and packed once your questionnaire is complete. You'll get a shipping notification as soon as your box is on its way.
